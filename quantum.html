Quantum Rush

Keep running till you can't no more!

Promotion video

In short:

  • During a school project, five fellow students and I worked for four weeks on the development of the game "Quantum Rush", a 3D Endless Runner with a futuristic Cyberpunk appearance that is very addictive and fun to play. In this game you are chased by a futuristic robot. Obstacles are strategically placed, and it's crucial to avoid them because after one touch the robot will grab you and your score will be saved and you'll have to start over. Within the game design we have integrated power-ups that we designed ourselves. Moreover, you can collect coins during the game to unlock different skins. An added feature is the leaderboard, where you can view the performance of other players.

Blender models

Blender models

I've been modeling various power-ups in Blender for the game. Some of these power-ups include double coins (2x Coins), a protective shield, an individual coin (Coin), and a magnet. These models add additional visual and functional elements to the game, allowing players to experience different benefits and challenges during their gameplay.

Scripts

  • I created several scripts for the game, including: Hover Script: This script is designed to make the items float smoothly from top to bottom when touched. It adds an attractive movement to the items and improves the overall gaming experience. Rotate Script: The rotate script is responsible for creating a rotating effect on the items. This causes the items to rotate on their axis, which is visually appealing and adds a dynamic element to the game design. Smooth Lane Switching Script: This script provides a smooth transition when the player changes lanes. Instead of an abrupt shift from one lane to another, this script makes the transition gradual and improves overall playability by minimizing unwanted jerks. These scripts contribute to the gaming experience by improving visual and functional aspects, making the game more attractive and user-friendly.
